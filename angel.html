Angel Food Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Strawberries
  • 2 Scoops Vanilla Ice Cream
  • 1 1/2 Cups Milk

Method

  1. Crush the strawberries in a blender.
  2. Add the ice cream and milk.
  3. Blend together until smooth.
  4. Serve with whipped cream!
