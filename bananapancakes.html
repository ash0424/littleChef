Banana Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 Medium Bananas
  • 1 Cup Oatmeal
  • 1 Cup Milk

Method

  1. Heat a non-stick pan over the stove on low heat.
  2. Thinly slice the bananas and add them into the blender.
  3. Add the oatmeal and milk.
  4. Blend all the ingredients together until smooth.
  5. Scoop the batter onto the non-stick pan and cook until brown on both sides.
  6. Serve with fresh bananas and honey!
