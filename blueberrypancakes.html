Blueberry Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Blueberries
  • 1 Cup Oatmeal
  • 1 Cup Milk

Method

  1. Heat a non-stick pan over the stove on low heat.
  2. Add the blueberries, oatmeal and milk into a blender.
  3. Blend all the ingredients together until smooth.
  4. Scoop the batter onto the non-stick pan and cook until brown on both sides.
  5. Serve with honey or maple syrup!
